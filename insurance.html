Integrated Physical Therapy Participates with these Insurance Networks

Integrated Physical Therapy's experienced and specialized therapists are patient centered with the goal of getting you back to your pain free function promptly. Please call your insurance to verify physical therapy coverage details and restrictions. We're happy to assist you with verifying in network and coverage specifics with your insurance.

Please contact our main office at 608-658-5352 for scheduling.
For billing or insurance questions please contact our billing office at (414)-434-8523 and speak with Maria directly.


We communicate with all your other medical providers for a team approach to care. Wisconsin is a direct access state, so depending on your insurance requirements you may be able to schedule your physical therapy visit without a referral from your doctor. 

If you do have a referral for physical therapy, your physician's office can send it directly to our

All known co-payments or deductibles will be collected at time of service. Any questions you have regarding your health insurance coverage or benefit levels should be directed to your health plan. We strongly advise that you check with your insurance company to fully understand the coverage provided through your insurance plan and any limitations for therapy services.
