Specialized PT Services

Integrated Physical Therapy's highly experienced and skilled manual physical therapists are patient-centered with the goal of getting you back to your pain free function promptly. We offer advanced and specialized physical therapy for treatment of injuries, chronic pain, orthopedic and spine issues. Our focus is on treating the cause of the condition, reducing postural or muscular imbalances and mechanical dysfunctions that promote healing for normal movement. Our approach is to normalize mobility of the joints and soft tissues with our extensive manual therapy (hands-on) techniques. We can then build the foundation of neuromuscular re-education and specific therapeutic exercises to promote stability, improve muscular strength, and maximize function.

  • Advanced Manual Therapy (hands on)
  • Soft tissue mobilization
  • Muscle energy
  • Myofascial release
  • Joint mobilization
  • Specialized Orthopedic Rehabilitation
  • Spine: neck, thoracic, lumbar, pelvis
  • Extremities: shoulder, elbow, wrist, hip, knee, foot
  • Chronic Pain, Fibromyalgia, Muscular Imbalance, Complex Pain Syndromes
  • Post Surgical and Total Joint Replacement Rehabilitation
  • Industrial Rehab, Work conditioning, and Ergonomics
  • Schroth Scoliosis and Kyphosis Treatment
  • Neural Mobilization Treatment After Sciatica or Radiculopathy
  • Advanced Neuromuscular Re-education
  • Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation (muscular retraining)
  • Visceral Manipulation
  • Biomechanical Evaluation
  • Walking and postural analysis
  • Custom foot orthotic fabrication
  • Expertise with Pilates, TRX certified, kettle bell, swiss ball, and functional stabalization exercises
  • Pelvic and Sacroiliac (SI) alignment
  • Senior physical therapy, arthritis and osteoporosis specific exercise
  • Dry Needling - Small needles to release trigger points
  • Functional Capacity Evaluations - Helpful to determine job function or daily abilities
  • Vestibular and balance training.
